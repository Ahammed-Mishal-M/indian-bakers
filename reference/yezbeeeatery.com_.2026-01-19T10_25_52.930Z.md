## Investment Options

## COCO (Company Owned Company Operated)

A fully owned and operated location managed by YezBee, ensuring brand consistency and quality control while providing a direct connection to our customers.

## FOCO (Franchise Owned Company Operated)

Franchisees own the outlet but operate under the guidance and brand standards of YezBee, benefiting from our established systems while retaining operational control.

## FICO (Franchise Invested Company Operated)

Franchisees invest in the business while YezBee manages operations, combining your investment with our expertise for a seamless operational experience.

## COFO (Company Owned Franchise Operated)

YezBee owns the outlet, while franchise partners manage daily operations, allowing for a blend of brand oversight with local operational insights.

## FOFO (Franchise Owned Franchise Operated)

This model empowers franchisees to fully own and operate their YezBee location, providing complete autonomy while still benefiting from our branding and support.
